Mystik Records

About

Mystik is a record label that specializes in releasing albums that have never seen a proper vinyl release, or have been out of print for a long time. The Mystik Records online store allows collectors and fans of niche artists and genres of music to discover albums that would otherwise be impossible to find in stores.
