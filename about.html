About Me

I’m a UX/UI Designer, Figma Wizard, and Curious Coder. My background is in Japanese language and literature, illustration, and graphic design. Most importantly, I’m a father to a 2-year-old named Edward, after Edward Scissorhands. I spent the first half of my twenties living in Tokyo, Japan, and the second half in Bologna, Italy. When not working, I like to paint, watch David Lynch movies, and compose music. I play saxophone, guitar, and synth. I also maintain a collection of rare Exotica, Jazz, Rockabilly, Light Music, and Vaporwave records.

About My Work

Designing for everyone begins by including everyone in the process. I enjoy collaborating with users, researching solutions, and solving problems in creative ways. I’m a speedy and iterative designer. I strive to make pixel-perfect, pretty, and palatable designs for all people. I am always seeking to maximize my efficiency by perfecting my workflow. I enjoy coding, and love learning more about this side of the creative process. Living overseas made me flexible and able to connect with many kinds of people. This influences the way I approach my designs and team relationships. In addition, my experience as a graphic designer has given me an eye for composition, color, and typography.
