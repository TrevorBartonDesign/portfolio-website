Hi, I'm Trevor.

About Me

I spent the first half of my twenties living in Tokyo, Japan, and the second half in Bologna, Italy. My time spent overseas taught me to be flexible, and how to communicate ideas succinctly to colleagues and users from diverse backgrounds. This influences the way I approach my designs. In addition, my past experience as an illustrator and graphic designer has given me a keen eye for composition, color, and typography.

About My Work

I’m a UX/UI Designer with a background in languages, literature, illustration, and graphic design. I’m a speedy and iterative worker, making pixel-perfect, pretty, and palatable designs for all people. I am fascinated by workflows, and am always seeking to maximize my efficiency. I'm one of the rare designers who also enjoys coding, and I love learning more about this side of the creative process.
